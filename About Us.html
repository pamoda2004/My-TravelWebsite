About Us – Island Trails Tours

Welcome to Island Trails Tours, your gateway to unforgettable journeys across the stunning island of Sri Lanka.We are a passionate team of travel experts dedicated to crafting immersive travel experiences that go beyond the ordinary.

Who We Are

Founded in 2017, Island Trails Tours is a locally-owned tourism company specializing in authentic, eco-friendly, and culture-rich travel across Sri Lanka. From the lush tea fields of Ella to the sacred city of Kandy, we connect travelers with the heart and soul of the island.

What We Offer...

  1. Guided cultural and historical tours

  2. Wildlife safaris and nature trails

  3. Adventure experiences: hiking, rafting, cycling

  4. Tailor-made travel packages

  5. Airport transfers and accommodation bookings
